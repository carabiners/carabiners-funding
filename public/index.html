A Focused Gateway to Early-Stage Capital

Carabiners is a startup funding facilitation platform designed to connect serious founders with private investors at the Angel and Seed stage.

We are not a public pitch marketplace and do not guarantee funding. Every application is screened before any investor introduction is considered.

This Platform Is For You If You Are:

  • An India-based startup or Indian founder
  • Raising Angel or Seed capital (₹5L – ₹1Cr)
  • Building an MVP, revenue-generating, or traction-ready business
  • Clear about your problem, solution, and market
  • Seeking strategic capital, not just money

Please Do Not Apply If:

  • You are only at the idea stage with no execution
  • You are looking for guaranteed funding
  • Your pitch relies only on buzzwords without substance
  • You are seeking grants, loans, or speculative capital

How Applications Are Evaluated

  • Problem clarity and urgency
  • Market size and scalability
  • Business model viability
  • Early traction or validation
  • Founder capability and commitment
  • Sensible use of capital

How the Process Works

  1. Apply: Submit your startup through a structured form
  2. Screening: Internal review for clarity and readiness
  3. Introduction: Shortlisted startups may be introduced to investors

Typical response time: 7–14 working days

Confidentiality & Transparency

All information shared is treated as confidential. Submitting your application does not guarantee funding. Carabiners acts solely as a facilitation platform and does not provide investment advice.
